Yamaha YZF 4-Stroke Dirt Bike Service Manuals


Yamaha is credited with popularizing the 4-stroke race bikes we know today. In 1998 Yamaha released the YZ400F to compete with the 250cc 2-strokes of the time. Soon after, displacement was bumped to 426cc, and eventually to 450cc in 2003. During the same time, in 2001, the YZ250F was offered to compete with the 125cc 2-strokes. Each bike has seen many improvements over the years, from aluminum frames to fuel injection and more, and Yamaha continues to improve each bike nearly every year.
