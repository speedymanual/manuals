Polaris Sportsman ATV Service Manuals


Polaris first introduced the Sportsman in 1993 as a 350cc 2-stroke model. They continued on as 2-strokes until 1995 when they switched to 4-stroke motors. Many different variations have been released in the years since, with many different motor sizes ranging from small to large. Advanced features such as fuel injection and power steering were added to some models. In 2009, the big-bore Sportsman 850 was added to the lineup, and in 2017, Polaris took it to the next level by adding the Sportsman XP 1000.
