Polaris Outlaw ATV Service Manuals


Polaris introduced their Outlaw line in 2006 as a replacement for the ending Predator line. The 2006-2007 500 models were completely new and built by Polaris, and was the first sport ATV to feature independent rear suspension (IRS) for more ground clearance and a better ride on the trails. In 2008, Polaris partnered with KTM to build the Outlaw 450 and 525 models, which were a step closer to race-ready machines, but still geared more toward fast trail riding. They were available with IRS and a solid rear axle. These full-size Outlaw models were last seen in the 2011 model year, but youth-sized 50 and 90/110 Outlaw models are still being made today.
