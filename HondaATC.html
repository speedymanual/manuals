Honda ATC 3-Wheeler Service Manuals


Honda is credited with inventing the 3-wheeled off-road vehicle in the late 1960s. The first model was the US90 which came in several bright colors with rimless balloon style tires. Other models soon followed, and Honda peaked in 1985/1986 with ten models available with motor sizes ranging from 70cc to 350cc. The king of the Honda utility trikes was the 1985-1987 Big Red 250es, while their sport lineup was ruled by the ATC250R 2-stroke for racing and ATC350X 4-stroke for fast trail riding. Sadly, a ban on production of new 3-wheeled machines put an end to the ATC line after 1987 models were released, but by that time Honda had already transitioned into the 4-wheeled ATV market and didn't miss a step.
