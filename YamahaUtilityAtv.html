Yamaha Utility ATV Service Manuals


Yamaha has been producing utility ATVs since the mid 1980s, with many different models available in both 2wd and 4x4 configurations. The original models were named simply Moto-4, and over the course of 10 years, 200, 225, 250 and 350 models were available in that line. Throughout the 1990s and into the 2000s other 250 models were available including the Timberwolf, Bear Tracker and Bruin. The Bruin was also available as a 350 model. In 1995 the popular Wolverine 350 was introduced, and it was upgraded to a 450 in 2006.
