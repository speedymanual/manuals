Suzuki Youth ATV Service Manuals


Suzuki was the originator of the 4-wheeled ATV in 1983 with their LT125. A year later they introduced the smaller LT50, which was made all the way into the 2000s. A couple variations of the 50 followed, both with added front suspension, and the later one with "Z" sport quad styling. The popular 2-stroke LT80 was added in 1987, and it was made for nearly 20 years, mostly without changes. It was finally replaced in 2007 by the LT-Z90 4-stroke, which is one of the few non-utility models that Suzuki currently produces.
