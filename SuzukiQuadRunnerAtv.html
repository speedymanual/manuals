Suzuki QuadRunner ATV Service Manuals


During the reign of 3-wheeled ATVs, Suzuki was "the first on 4 wheels" with the introduction of the LT125 in 1983. Coined the QuadRunner line, several other variations were released through the 1980s and 1990s, with a mix of sport and utility machines to suit most buyers. The top of the line QuadRunner 500 had a run from 1998 to 2002, and paved the way for future Suzuki big bore machines like the Vinson and KingQuad. While the QuadRunner name is no longer used by Suzuki, their heritage lives on.
