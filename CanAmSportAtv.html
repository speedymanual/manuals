Can-Am / Bombardier Sport ATV Service Manuals


Bombardier introduced their first sport ATV, the DS650, in 2000. Upon its release it would compete with the Honda TRX400ex and upcoming Yamaha Raptor 660 for top sport ATV honors. The DS650 continued on through the 2007 model year, and it was dropped from the lineup in 2008 in favor of the new DS450. The DS450 was created of course to compete with the other sport 450s already available. While being considered close to race ready out of the box, the DS450 was more at home on the trails than the track. Unfortunately the DS450 has been missing from the Can-Am sport ATV lineup since 2016.
