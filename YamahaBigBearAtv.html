Yamaha Big Bear ATV Service Manuals


The Yamaha Big Bear line arrived in 1987 as an alternative to the Moto-4 350 line. It featured a 350cc model until 1999, and in 2000 motor displacement was bumped to 400cc. The Big Bear 250 was added for three short model years, from 2007 to 2009. The Big Bear line was discontinued after the 2012 model year.
