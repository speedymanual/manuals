Yamaha WR Dirt Bike Service Manuals


Yamaha's popular WR line has been available for many years as a alternative to their YZ and YZF race bike models. Like most others, they started out as 2-strokes and have transitioned to modern 4-strokes over time. They are very simliar to their race-bike counterparts, with an added headlight, taillight, kick stand and transmission revisions geared more for trail riding. WR models don't receive updates nearly as often as the YZF race bikes.
