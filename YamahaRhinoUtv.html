Yamaha Rhino UTV Service Manuals


When Yamaha introduced the Rhino 660 in 2004, it changed the landscape of the UTV market. Before 2004, UTVs were mostly known as commercial machines, with the majority of them being sold to businesses. With the introduction of the Rhino, consumer UTVs finally surpassed commercial sales, and the gap has only grown since. Yamaha bumped the Rhino motor displacement to 700 in 2008, and a smaller 450 model was introduced in 2006. While the Rhino has been discontinued in favor of the Viking, it will go down in history as a game changer.
