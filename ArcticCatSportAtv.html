Arctic Cat Sport ATV Service Manuals


Arctic Cat was one of the last major manufacturers to get into the sport ATV market. Known primarily as a utility ATV manufacturer, it took a joint effort with Suzuki to get into the game in a major way with the DVX400 in 2004. Much like the Kawasaki KFX 400, the Arctic Cat DVX400 was a rebadged Suzuki Z400, but with styling that many agreed looked the best of the three models. Later in the 2000s the DVX250 and DVX300 were added to the lineup. Each of these models have a sporty look, but are more or less utility ATVs underneath. In 2011 the XC 450 was introduced as a sport/utility crossover ATV featuring sporty styling and a utility-like CVT transmission.
