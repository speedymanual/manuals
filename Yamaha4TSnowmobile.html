Yamaha Snowmobile 4-Stroke Service Manuals


Yamaha entered the 4-stroke snowmobile market in 2003, with the introduction of its RX-1, which many consider to be the first performance 4-stroke snowmobile by any manufacturer. Since then, Yamaha's 4-stroke models have become much more advanced, and continue to improve year after year, to the point that they have left the 2-stroke snowmobile market. Around 2014 Yamaha began a partnership with Arctic Cat to provide them with 4-stroke motors in exchange for the use of Cat sled chassis.
