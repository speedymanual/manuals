Suzuki Utility ATV Service Manuals


Since the late 1980s Suzuki has been offering utility ATVs in several different variations. The QuadRunners of the 1980s and 1990s gave way to the Eiger 400 and Vinson 500 in 2002. Each of these models were available with automatic or foot shift transmissions. In 2004 Suzuki got into the big-bore game with the Twin Peaks 700, which came to exist in another joint effort with Kawasaki. It is a rebadged Kawasaki Prairie 700. The Eiger, Vinson and Twin Peaks were eventually discontinued as the KingQuad was rising in the Suzuki lineup.
