Yamaha YZ 2-Stroke Dirt Bike Service Manuals


The Yamaha YZ line was introduced way back in the 1970s, around the same time the other Japanese manufacturers were releasing their own 2-stroke MX line. The YZ80 was available until 2001, and it became the YZ85 for the 2002 model year. The YZ125 and YZ250 have seen many variations and color schemes over the last 40 years. Surprisingly, Yamaha still offers both models even though they have not been updated in many years. They are the only Japanese manufacturer with 125 or 250 2-stroke bikes in their lineup.
