Yamaha Youth ATV Service Manuals


Yamaha has been in the youth ATV market since the late 1980s, with such models as the 4-Zinger, Badger 80, Champ 100 and Breeze 125. Later on in the 2000s, sportier models like the Raptor 50 and Raptor 80 were added. The Raptor 80 gave way to the Raptor 90 in 2009. Youth-sized Grizzly models were even added to the mix, in the Grizzly 80 and Grizzly 125. For 2018 Yamaha has introduced the smallest YFZ to the lineup, the YFZ50.
