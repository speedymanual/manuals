Yamaha Youth Dirt Bike Service Manuals


Yamaha has been producing 2-stroke and 4-stroke youth bikes for many years. The TT-R lineup features several different 4-stroke models, with youth bike sizes in 50, 70, 90, 110 and 125 models. When young riders get more experienced they will normally move up to the YZ80/YZ85 2-stroke line. Yamaha made the YZ80 for many years, and now offers the YZ85. While it hasn't been updated for many years, the YZ85 is still a favorite for young riders learning to ride fast.
