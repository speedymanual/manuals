Yamaha Kodiak ATV Service Manuals


The Yamaha Kodiak line first appeared in 1993 as a 400cc model. It continued on as a 400 with minimal changes until 2005. During the same time period, a 450cc model was made from 2003-2006, and then the line was discontinued in favor of other utility models. In 2016, Yamaha revived the Kodiak name, this time as a new 700cc model that is a less expensive alternative to Yamaha's own Grizzly 700.
