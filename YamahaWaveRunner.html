Yamaha WaveRunner PWC Service Manuals


Yamaha introduced its WaveRunner PWC line for the 1987 model year. Originally all models were 2-strokes, but like other manufacturers they transitioned into building 4-stroke models midway through the 2000s. Along the way advanced features have been added, like supercharged motors and GPS. Yamaha still offers new WaveRunner models each year.
