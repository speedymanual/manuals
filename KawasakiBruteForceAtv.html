Kawasaki Brute Force ATV Service Manuals


The Kawasaki Brute Force was first introduced in 2005 as a replacement for the large displacement Prairie line. Both 650 and 750 models were available the first year. The Brute Force 750 quickly became Kawasaki's top of the line ATV, and remains so today. In 2012 Kawasaki released a 300cc Brute Force model to compete in the small utility ATV market that other manufacturers have had success in.
