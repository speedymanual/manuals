KTM 250/300 2-Stroke Dirt Bike Service Manuals


KTM has been making 250cc and 300cc 2-stroke bikes for many years. While not as popular as the Japanese bikes during the 250 motocross days, KTM hit a homerun with the introduction of the 300 for off-road trail riding. In 2008 the 300 received electric start, revolutionizing the 2-stroke market in the process. KTM is one of the few manufacturers still offering and even updating 2-stroke bikes.
