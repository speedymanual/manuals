Suzuki RM-Z 4-Stroke Dirt Bike Service Manuals


Suzuki joined in the 4-stroke MX scene in 2004 with their RM-Z250. This was another bike created via their joint venture with Kawasaki. The motor was designed and built by Suzuki while the chassis was built was Kawasaki. The Kawasaki KX250F was the same bike with green plastic. This alliance lasted until 2006 and since 2007 the RM-Z250 has been made entirely by Suzuki. In 2005 the RM-Z450 was released, a year or two after other manufacturers had released their own 450 MX bikes. Each model continues to receive regular updates, with fuel injection being added to the 450 in 2008 and to the 250 in 2010.
