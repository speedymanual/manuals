Kawasaki Prairie ATV Service Manuals


The Kawasaki Prairie was first introduced in 1997 as a 400cc model. The smaller Prairie 300 followed two years later, and it was eventually replaced by the popular 360 model in 2003. Larger 650 and 700 Prairie models were offered from 2002-2006. The Prairie line came into popularity as the previous generation Bayou line was phased out.
