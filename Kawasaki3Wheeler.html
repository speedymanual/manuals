Kawasaki 3-Wheeler Service Manuals


While not as popular as the hugely successful Honda ATC line, Kawasaki released several 4-stroke 3-wheelers in the 1980s, and one incredibly fast sport trike. The 4-stroke models were made from 1981-1985, and featured motor sizes of 110, 160, 200 and 250. They were reliable and meant for work or play like the other 4-strokes of the time. The first generation Tecate 250 was no slouch, but in 1986 they swung for the fences with a completely new model, aimed directly at the Honda ATC250R. The 1986-1987 Tecate 250 was known for its massive top speed but lacked the sharp handling that the Honda possessed.
