Polaris Sport ATV Service Manuals


Polaris entered the sport ATV market in the early 2000s with their Predator and Outlaw models. The other models that followed straddled the line of sport and utility. The Phoenix 200 has been a popular choice for younger or less experienced riders. The popular TrailBlazer and Scrambler feature sporty styling, 4x4 and a CVT transmission so anybody can ride them. In 2013 Polaris redesigned the Scrambler and took it to a new level by adding an 850cc motor, and the following year a 1000cc model was added to the lineup.
