Honda Youth Dirt Bike Service Manuals


Honda has been producing youth dirt bikes for over 50 years, dating back to the 1960s. Their small models like the Z50A and Z50R were very popular, and continued on for many years with minimal changes. From the 1970s to the early 2000s, the XR line provided the youth models, with models such as the XR50, XR70, XR80 and XR100. Once the XR line was phased out, the CRF line was next to provide youth models, with the CRF50F, CRF70F, CRF80F, CRF100F, CRF110F and CRF125F models taking over youth duties.
