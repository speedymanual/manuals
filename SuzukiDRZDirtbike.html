Suzuki DR/DR-Z 4-Stroke Dirt Bike Service Manuals


The Suzuki DR line has been around since the early 1990s, with several models available. The lineup includes Suzuki's trail bikes, as well as some dual sport bikes. The DR line eventually gave way to the DR-Z line, introduced in the early 2000s and still made today. There have been a range of bike sizes, from the youth-sized DR-Z70 and DR-Z100 up to the popular DR-Z400 and DR650.
