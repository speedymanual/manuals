Honda CRF 4-Stroke Dirt Bike Service Manuals


The Honda CRF line of dirt bikes was introduced in the early 2000s as a successor to the XR line of bikes. While the XR line featured primarily trail bikes (with race bike duties covered by the CR line), the CRF line features both trail bikes and the CRF150R, CRF250R and CRF450R MX race bikes. Honda was one of the first manufacturers to offer a 450R race bike, introducing its version in 2002. The 250F model followed two years later. Each bike still receives regular updates. Also in the CRF line are two dual sport bikes, the CRF230L and its replacement, the CRF250L.
