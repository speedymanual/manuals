Yamaha Snowmobile 2-Stroke Service Manuals


Yamaha has been manufacturing snowmobiles since the late 1960s, and for about the first 40 years, they were all 2-strokes. They had many popular fan-cooled models throughout the 1980s and early 1990s, with models such as the Enticer, Exciter and Phazer. Once the mid 1990s hit, models became faster and more advanced, with 700cc, 750cc and 800cc motors, some with three and four cylinders. Yamaha has mostly left the 2-stroke snowmobile market, with the exception of some of its holdover models like the VK 540.
