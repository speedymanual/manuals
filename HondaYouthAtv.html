Honda Youth ATV Service Manuals


Honda has been making ATVs for the younger crowd since the late 1980s. Several of their youth models were created by more or less adding a fourth wheel to their already successful 3-wheeled models. The TRX70 replaced the ATC70, and the TRX125 was made at the same time as the ATC125M, and eventually replaced it. The popular TRX90 originally appeared in 1993, and is the only youth ATV Honda still makes today.
