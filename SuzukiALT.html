Suzuki ALT 3-Wheeler Service Manuals


Suzuki first started making their ALT 3-wheeled machine line in 1983, which was also the year they became the first manufacturer to produce a 4-wheeled atv. The first ALT models were the 50 and 125, and eventually a 185 model was added in 1985 for that year only. By 1987 the ALT models were gone from the Suzuki lineup, around the same time as the other manufacturers as the focus turned toward 4-wheelers. Today, the ALT line lags in popularity among 3-wheeler enthusiasts, far behind Honda and Kawasaki models.
