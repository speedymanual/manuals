Kawasaki KXF 4-Stroke Dirt Bike Service Manuals


Kawasaki entered the 250F market in 2004 in a joint effort with Suzuki. Suzuki provided the motor and Kawasaki provided the chassisa and built the bike. Since 2007 the KX250F has been made entirely by Kawasaki. Like their entry into the 450 sport ATV market, Kawasaki was three to four years behind the other manufacturers in releasing their KX450F in 2006. Each bike has seen many improvements over the years, from aluminum frames to fuel injection and more, and Kawasaki continues to improve each bike nearly every year.
