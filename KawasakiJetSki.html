Kawasaki Jet-Ski PWC Service Manuals


Kawasaki revolutionized the PWC market with the release of the first Jet-Ski in 1973. Over the years the name Jet-Ski has become synonymous with the term personal watercraft, even though only Kawasaki PWCs are truly Jet-Skis. Along with the other major manufacturers they began investing in the 4-stroke market around 2003. Before that all models were 2-strokes. Kawasaki is the only major PWC manufacturer that still offers a stand-up model, the legendary SX-R. It made a return in 2017 as a 1000cc 4-stroke after being missing from the lineup since 2012. Kawasaki continues to improve and advance the Jet-Ski line year after year with no slowdown in sight.
