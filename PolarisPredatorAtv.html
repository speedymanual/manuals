Polaris Predator ATV Service Manuals


Polaris entered the sport ATV market in a big way in 2003 with their Predator 500. Built as a competitor to other sport models such as the Honda TRX400ex, Suzuki Z400 and Yamaha Raptor 660, it was a respectable first entry into the market. Youth-sized 50 and 90 models soon followed before the line was discontinued in favor of the new Outlaw models.
