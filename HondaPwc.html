The Honda Aquatrax line had a brief run from 2003-2007, and was discontinued after sales were lower than Honda had hoped for. After getting into the market much later than other manufacturers, Honda was playing catch up from day one, and never earned a large share of the market. For their time they were nice units, with features such as a powerful 4-stroke turbocharged motor, and reverse.
